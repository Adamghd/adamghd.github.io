ABOUT

You’ve seen it, “Do what you love and love what you do”. These have always been our words to live by. We both went to college, but over the years, never really found the “dream job.” We are a family of four, all very active and energetic in life, constantly craving a new adventure.
Our love of travel can most likely be attributed to our parents and the many adventures we both had growing up. We were fortunate to travel around the U.S. and overseas on multiple occasions. We met in 1995 and by 1998 travelled together to the UK, Scotland, Ireland, and backpacked all over Europe. The journeys continue, with the love of travel deep in our hearts today. Erik and I want to give our children all the opportunities we had and more. With our family's passion for the outdoors, other cultures, and thirst for adventure, it only seemed fitting to start a travel business. BE Adventurous Travel allows us to share with you, our love of seeing the world.
Through the many ups and downs of life, we’ve certainly learned that things are unpredictable. Don’t wait until retirement, one more year, or the “perfect” time. Life is short, so make the most of it. Instead of a “bucket list”, the Farrar Family has a “Dream List”. If you haven’t already, start your dream list, and call or email us today. Whether it’s a getaway weekend, road trip, family reunion, or an exotic vacation to a faraway land, we can help make your dreams a reality.
